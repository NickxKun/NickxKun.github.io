About Me

Hello! I am Nikhil Kunjoor, a dedicated engineer specializing in hardware design and computer engineering. With a Master's in Computer Engineering from Arizona State University and a B.Tech in Electronics and Communication from PES University, I have a strong foundation in both theory and practice. My experience spans across designing and validating hardware, developing sophisticated software for system testing, and creating innovative projects in areas such as AI inference and microarchitecture verification.

I thrive on challenges and continuously seek opportunities to innovate and push the boundaries of technology.
